What Is Nuclear Warfare?

A nuclear war is a war in which different countries fight with nuclear weapons. Because nuclear weapons are extremely powerful and could cause destruction throughout the world, the possibility of nuclear war has a bigger affect on politics.

There are three types of nuclear weapons:

  1. Nuclear Fusion is the first type of nuclear weapons.

  2. Nuclear Fission is the second.

  3. Radioactive Decay is the third and most powerful.

Nuclear Fusion:

Nuclear Fusion makes two small atomic nuclei into one nucleus.

Nuclear Fission:

Nuclear Fission splits one larde nucleus into smaller fragments

Radioactive Decay

The change of a less stable nuclus to a stable nucleus
